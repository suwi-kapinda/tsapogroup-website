Massbreed Investment Zimbabwe

Massbreed Investments (t/a FAW Zimbabwe) is a Zimbabwean commercial vehicle provider founded in January 2004, offering FAW trucks and Chinese-manufactured buses adapted for local terrain. Registered with the Motor Industry Association of Zimbabwe, it caters to government, mining, construction, and transport sectors. The company operates showrooms in Harare and Bulawayo, complete with customer lounges and service bays.

Our Commercial Vehicle Services Include:

  • • FAW Trucks Sales & Service
  • • Chinese-Manufactured Buses
  • • Local Terrain Adaptation
  • • Vehicle Maintenance & Repair
  • • Parts & Accessories Supply

  • • Fleet Management Solutions
