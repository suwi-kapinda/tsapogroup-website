Lovol Zimbabwe

Lovol Zimbabwe, operating under the Tsapo Group since 2018, is the authorized distributor of Lovol agricultural, construction, and mining machinery. It stocks a wide array of equipment including tractors, excavators, graders, and combine harvesters designed to meet Zimbabwe's diverse terrain and operational demands. The company supports operations with genuine spare parts and robust after-sales services like maintenance and repair.
