About Us

About Tsapo Group of Companies

Founded in 2013, Tsapo Group of Companies is a proudly Zimbabwean and regionally recognized diversified conglomerate. We are home to over 15 dynamic Strategic Business Units (SBUs), each committed to delivering exceptional value across a wide spectrum of industries. With a solid foundation built on innovation, operational excellence, and a customer-centric approach, our expertise spans across motor dealership, logistics, insurance, risk management, real estate, property management, financial services, public and private transport solutions, industrial manufacturing, ICT, and travel and tour services. Driven by a unified vision to shape industry standards and empower economic progress, Tsapo Group stands as a trusted corporate partner for clients, communities, and stakeholders alike. Our relentless pursuit of service excellence, sustainable growth, and ethical business practices continues to position us at the forefront of Zimbabwe's evolving business landscape

Sectors We Serve.

We believe in creating value for our clients and customer service is the pinnacle of our success throughout the years. Excellence is at the core of our values

Zimbabwe

Agriculture

Tsapo group is a key player in the agricultural sector with equipment and vehicle supply, including but not limited to Tractors, Combine Harvesters, Center Pivots, Seed Drills, Disk Harrows.

Mining & Construction

Tsapo Group in conjunction with Lovol, brings turnkey solutions for mineral processing providing safe and sustainable drill rigs, excavators, loaders, rock excavation and construction tools and equipment.

Transport & Logistics

Tsapo Group provides reliable and efficient Transport & Logistics solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of its clients. With a modern fleet and skilled personnel, the company ensures safe and timely movement of goods across local and regional routes.

Insurance

Tsapo Group offers a range of Insurance services to protect clients and their assets. From travel insurance to vehicle and personal cover, the company provides comprehensive solutions that bring peace of mind. By combining affordability with reliability, Tsapo ensures clients are safeguarded against risks

Travel & Tours

Tsapo Group offers exceptional Travel and Tour services, designed to provide clients with safe, comfortable, and memorable experiences. The company specializes in both local and regional travel, offering tailored packages for leisure, business, and group tours.

Information Technology

Tsapo Group also extends its expertise into Information Technology (IT) services, helping businesses and individuals embrace digital transformation. The company offers solutions in systems integration, IT support, software development.
